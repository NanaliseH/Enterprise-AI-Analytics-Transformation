# TechNova Inc.
## Executive Summary: Enterprise AI & Analytics Transformation

**Prepared by:** Analytics Centre of Excellence
**Date:** June 2026
**Classification:** Confidential – Board & C-Suite

---

### Business Problem

TechNova Inc. faces three converging pressures: a **23.7% customer churn rate** that is eroding annual recurring revenue, a **support operations backlog** driving 10.3% ticket escalations and 28-hour average resolution times, and an **AI adoption rate of only 36.9%** — well below the 50% strategic target — limiting the productivity gains that justified our technology investment roadmap.

Left unaddressed, these factors are projected to suppress revenue growth by 8–12% and reduce profit margins by 3–5 percentage points over the next 12 months.

---

### Key Findings

**Customer Analytics**
- Churn rate of **23.7%** (vs. 18% industry benchmark). Month-to-month contract holders churn at nearly 3× the rate of two-year contract customers.
- Customers with **Fiber Optic internet and Electronic Check payment** represent the highest-churn cohort.
- Average Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) is **$2,442** — meaning each churned customer costs approximately this amount in lost revenue.
- The top five churn predictors (Random Forest model, AUC = 0.766) are: **tenure, monthly charges, contract type, internet service type, and tech support access**.

**Financial Performance**
- Revenue has grown from $1.18M to **$1.42M/month** over 24 months (+20.3%).
- Profit margin averages **36.4%** in the latest quarter, above the 30% target, but trending slightly downward due to rising support costs.
- 6-month revenue forecast: **$1.62M → $1.72M/month**, assuming no major market disruption.

**Operational Efficiency**
- Support ticket volume has grown 12% YoY, with average resolution time at **28.4 hours**.
- Escalation rate of **10.3%** is above the 7% target, indicating insufficient front-line resolution capability.

**Social Media & Brand**
- Average sentiment score: **0.592 (positive)**, and improving (+0.004/month trend).
- **LinkedIn and Twitter/X** deliver highest engagement (7.8% and 7.7% respectively).
- **Thought Leadership content** outperforms all other post types in engagement.

**Risk Profile**
- **Low AI Adoption Risk = HIGH** (score: 0.457) — most urgent risk requiring immediate executive action.
- Negative Sentiment Risk = MEDIUM (0.344); Support Backlog Risk = MEDIUM (0.340).
- Revenue Decline Risk = LOW (0.058) — financial trajectory is healthy near-term.

### Scenario & Investment Recommendations

Three investment scenarios were modelled over a 6-month horizon:

| Scenario | Investment | Total Benefit | ROI |
|---|---|---|---|
| A: Increase Marketing Spend | $480,000 | $889,014 | **85.2%** |
| B: AI Customer Support Assistant | $320,000 | $774,704 | **142.1%** ✓ |
| C: Hire Additional Support Staff | $620,000 | $387,508 | **-37.5%** |

**★ Recommendation: Scenario B — Deploy an AI Customer Support Assistant**

With an ROI of 142% in 6 months, Scenario B delivers the highest return at the lowest capital outlay. It directly addresses the top two risks (low AI adoption and support backlog), projects a 9% reduction in churn, and reduces ticket volume by 35%. It also accelerates the internal AI adoption KPI toward the 50% strategic target.
